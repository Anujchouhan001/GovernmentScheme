About Scheme Finder

A College Project for Social Good

Project Overview

Scheme Finder is an intelligent web application designed to help citizens of Bihar discover government schemes they are eligible for. Many people are unaware of the welfare schemes available to them due to scattered information and complex eligibility criteria.

This project bridges that gap by providing a simple questionnaire-based approach that matches users with relevant schemes based on their profile.

Key Features

  • 106 Government Schemes
  • Smart Matching Algorithm
  • Score-based Ranking
  • Detailed Scheme Info
  • Direct Application Links
  • Mobile Responsive

How the Matching Works

Our algorithm uses multiple matching criteria:

Criteria Match Type Points
Age Range Match (e.g., 18-50) 20 pts
Gender Exact Match 15 pts
Category Exact Match (SC/ST/BC/EBC) 15 pts
Occupation Keyword Match 15 pts
Income Range Match (Below limit) 10 pts
BPL Status Exact Match 10 pts
Disability Exact Match 10 pts
Keywords Text Search 5 pts
Total Maximum Score 100 pts
